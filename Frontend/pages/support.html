Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an investment?
Navigate to the Investment page, select your investment type, choose a business, enter the amount, and submit your investment request.
How long does it take to process an investment?
Investment processing typically takes 3-5 business days. You'll receive email notifications about the status of your investment.
Can I withdraw my investment?
Investment withdrawal policies depend on the specific investment terms. Please contact our support team for detailed information.
How do I update my profile information?
Go to Profile Settings to update your personal information, contact details, and account preferences.
How do I connect with other users?
Use the search feature to find other users, then send them a connection request. Once accepted, you can message them directly.
Is my personal information secure?
Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your personal and financial information. We never share your data with third parties without your consent.
Need Immediate Assistance?
For urgent matters outside business hours, please send us an email and we'll respond as soon as possible. For critical issues, you can also reach us through our social media channels.
